Our Story


One fateful day in August 2011, Dayna came across a Craigslist ad for an open room in San Francisco. She didn't get the spot for the roommate, but she got Matt's attention...

Matt knew he was hooked when, at the EA Christmas party a few months later, Dayna busted out dance moves that put his own to shame. Her grace and goofiness, athleticism and attitude, passion and love, thoughtfullness and faith all melded together to create the most beautiful woman he ever met.

Dayna knew that Matt was the one when Dayna took a nasty spill during an icy day of snowboarding and she wanted nobody else but Matt to take care of her. Matt then ran around the village in search of candy, beers and advil to make the stay in the doctor's office more enjoyable. Dayna fell for his amazing charm, love for life's adventures, passion, faith, silliness and family values. He was the man she had been praying for since she was a little girl.

Matt sealed the deal while on a romantic wine-tasting trip in Napa. Little did Dayna know, Matt planned for both of their immediate families and closest friends to be at the next winery. Photos of their engagement day can be found here.
